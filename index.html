Need help?
  1. Start by adding an activity using the Log Activity form above to log your carbon footprint.
  2. When you focus your cursor on the activity input field, a list of suggested activities will appear based on your input and you can choose any of them.
  3. If your activity isn’t found in the system, you’ll be prompted to define it manually and provide its CO₂ emission factor for future use.
